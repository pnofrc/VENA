VENA is a non-profit association established in 2019 after two years of work as cultural circle rooted within an old ceramics factory on the outskirts of East Milan, contributing actively to the development of the underground framework via Tucidide.
Growing like the veins of a multidisciplinary organism, it ramifies through various interests and projections of the natures of its participants;
relying on independent projects it aims at pursuing a common goal: a sprawling culture characterized by a strong desire to represent a multifaceted spirit of disciplinary commitment and constructive fun.
